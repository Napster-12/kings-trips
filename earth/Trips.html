📜 Our Journey & History

From small beginnings to creating lifetime memories

2022

The Beginning

KingSTrips was founded by Thulani Blessing Mathebula with a vision to create safe, affordable group travel experiences. Our first trip was a small school tour with just 15 students to Durban.

🎯 First trip: 15 travellers 📍 Durban
2023

Growing Fast

Expanded to 5 successful trips including our first church group tour to Cape Town. Started getting recognition for professional service and attention to detail.

🏆 50+ happy travellers 📍 Cape Town 📍 Durban
2024

First International Trip

Successfully organised our first cross-border trip to Maputo, Mozambique. Started offering corporate retreats and team-building packages.

🌍 First international trip 📍 Maputo 💼 Corporate packages
2025

Major Milestone

Reached 100+ happy travellers! Introduced our exclusive small-group international trips. Started the Namibia tours which became our most popular package.

🎉 100+ travellers Maputo tours ⭐ 4.8 star rating
2026

Now & Beyond

Planning bigger and better trips! Now offering 5+ major destinations, school trips, church tours, corporate retreats, and custom packages. Our community keeps growing.
